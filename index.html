A Perfect Day: The Stops

Little Goat Cafe

Start your trip with a visit to Little Goat. The mix of traditional American breakfast staples with asian-inspired kicks is sure to leave you delighted and surprised!

820 W Randolph St, Chicago, IL 60607

Garretts

After walking-off some of the breakfast carbs, refuel by eating delicious caramel-covered cheddar popcorn! This sweet and salty combination is best consumed shortly after being popped, while its still warm and gooey.

600 E Grand Ave, Chicago, IL 60611

Portillos

For $12 you get a double-pint of beer, all-beef hotdog with fixins, and cheese fries. 'Nuff said.

100 W Ontario St, Chicago, IL 60654

Intelligensia

For an afternoon refuel, stop by this classic hipster-too-cool-to-give-you-decent-customer-service-vibe place. But hurry, before Target copies the vibe and reduces to oat milk to mainstream status.

3123 N Broadway Chicago, IL 60657

Sunda

Chicago's hottest club is Sunda. The see-and-be-seen crowd devours decadent sushi at this fusion gastrobar. Make sure you're not near your credit limit before paying!

110 W Illinois St, Chicago, IL 60654
